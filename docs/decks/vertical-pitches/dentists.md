## Built for the regulator your patients trust

Every published change is checked against the rules that govern your practice — automatically, before it ships:

- **RCDSO / state-dental-board advertising-policy lint.** Comparative claims, superlatives, before/after photos without a documented consent log, unsupported outcome claims — all flagged before publish. The compliance call stays yours; we do the pre-screen.
- **AODA / ADA accessibility compliance.** Lighthouse + axe-core run on every preview build. If accessibility regresses, the build fails — and you don't see a broken site reach a patient.
- **PHIPA / HIPAA-aware contact form architecture.** The form rejects PHI capture by default. Clinical questions are routed back to your booking system. We don't become a covered entity.
- **Audit log dated for your file.** Every accessibility scan, every content change, every advertising-policy review — recorded and retrievable.

Compliance becomes a property of the system, not a property of your office manager's memory.

Transparency, not bait-and-switch. We don't:
