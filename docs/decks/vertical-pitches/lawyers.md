## Built for the regulator your bar takes seriously

Every published change is checked against the rules that govern your practice — automatically, before it ships:

- **LSO §4.2 / state-bar 7.1 advertising-policy lint.** Comparative claims, "expert" / "specialist" without LSO-Specialist designation, testimonials about specific cases, guaranteed outcomes — all flagged before publish. You decide; we pre-screen.
- **AODA / ADA accessibility compliance.** Lighthouse + axe-core run on every preview build. If accessibility regresses, the build fails. You don't see a broken site reach a prospect.
- **PIPEDA / state-privacy aware contact form architecture.** The form rejects privileged-info capture by default. The auto-responder reminds prospects not to include privileged information until conflict-cleared.
- **Required-disclosure block (firm name, address, LSO / state-bar registration, jurisdictional limitations) as a structural site footer.** Phone-edit-able where allowed; locked where mandated.
- **Audit log dated for your file.** Every accessibility scan, every advertising-policy review, every privacy review — recorded and retrievable for your insurer or LSO file.
