## What an outdated optometry site costs you, today

You already feel some of this:

- **Your optical retail / frame display online is stale.** You carry 200–500 frames in the dispensary; the site shows 30 from 2021. Patients shopping by frame brand bounce when the catalogue is incomplete.
- **OHIP coverage explanation is missing.** Ontario covers annual eye exams for ages 0–19 and 65+; many sites either don't mention this or have it buried. Parents shopping for paediatric exams pick the practice that surfaces the answer.
- **Insurance direct-billing list is incomplete.** Sun Life, Manulife, GreenShield, Canada Life — the practice direct-bills 8–12; the site says "most major insurers."
- **Optical retail revenue is leaking online.** Warby Parker / GlassesUSA / Zenni / 1-800-Contacts are eating the dispensary side. Without a real online frame catalogue + virtual try-on, every percentage point of online-optical share is a percentage point of your retail revenue.
- **Specialty pages are weak.** Vision therapy, low vision, sports / performance vision — you've invested in equipment but the site doesn't reflect the depth.
- **Reviews are stagnant.** Independent practices typically lag corporate by 5–20× in review count.

## Built for the regulator your college recognises

Every published change is checked against the rules that govern your practice — automatically, before it ships:

- **COO / state-board advertising-policy lint.** Comparative claims, outcome guarantees, "free eye exam" without conditions disclosed, scope-of-practice overreach — all flagged before publish.
- **FTC Contact Lens Rule (Tinkler-Boozman / 16 CFR Part 315) lint** for US engagements: every CL fitting / reorder page is checked for the prescription-release disclosure. The lint flags; you decide.
- **AODA / ADA accessibility compliance.** Lighthouse + axe-core run on every preview build. If accessibility regresses, the build fails.
- **PIPEDA / state-privacy aware contact form architecture.** The form rejects vision-history capture by default; clinical questions auto-redirect to your booking system.
- **Audit log dated for your file.** Every accessibility scan, every advertising-policy review, every privacy review — recorded and retrievable for your COO file.
